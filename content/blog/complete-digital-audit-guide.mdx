Your business might be the best HVAC company in Texarkana. Your team might be the most skilled, your pricing the most fair, your service the most reliable.

But if potential customers can't find you on Google, if your reviews don't reflect your quality, and if your online presence is disorganized — none of that matters. Because they'll call your competitor instead.

We've conducted digital audits on hundreds of service businesses across Texas. The average score? **35%**.

That means the vast majority of service businesses are leaving 50-80% of their online potential on the table. Here's what that score actually means, what's dragging it down, and how to fix it.

## The 5 Categories We Audit

### 1. Business Details (35% of score)

This category checks the foundational elements of your online identity:

- **Is your phone SMS-enabled?** — 90% of businesses fail this check. Customers want to text you.
- **Is your hosting detected?** — 70% use unknown hosts that Google doesn't trust.
- **Do you have a chat widget?** — 85% don't. Chat converts 40% more visitors.
- **Are you replying to reviews?** — 95% aren't. Google rewards responsiveness.

**Average score in this category: 15%**

### 2. Techno Stack (20% of score)

This measures your marketing technology foundation:

- **Google Analytics** — 65% of businesses don't have it installed. You can't improve what you don't measure.
- **Google Tag Manager** — Manages all tracking codes without editing website code. 80% don't have it.
- **Google Ads conversion tracking** — Without it, 30-50% of ad spend is blind.
- **Meta (Facebook) Pixel** — Enables retargeting. 75% don't have it installed.

**Average score in this category: 8%**

### 3. Google Business Profile (15% of score)

Your GB Profile is the most important piece of your online presence:

- **Photos** — Profiles with 20+ photos get 2x more views. Most have fewer than 5.
- **Services** — Only 30% list all their services with correct categories.
- **Hours** — 40% have incorrect or incomplete hours.
- **Q&A** — 85% have no pre-answered questions.

**Average score in this category: 45%**

### 4. Directory Listings (15% of score)

Your business appears on dozens of platforms — Google, Yelp, Facebook, Bing, Apple Maps, and more:

- **NAP consistency** — 85% of businesses have conflicting Name, Address, or Phone across directories.
- **Incomplete profiles** — Most platforms have missing or outdated information.
- **Google's signal conflict** — Inconsistent data across directories confuses Google and lowers your ranking.

**Average score in this category: 18%**

### 5. Reputation (15% of score)

The reviews and ratings that customers see when they find you:

- **Too few reviews** — Average service business has 12 reviews. Target is 50+ for 3x more calls.
- **No response to negatives** — 90% ignore negative reviews. 30% of customers will update their review after a good response.

**Average score in this category: 38%**

## What These Numbers Mean for Your Business

A score of 35% means your business is visible online, but barely. You're losing leads to competitors who have done the work to optimize their presence.

Here's a rough guide to what each score range means for your monthly lead volume:

- **0-20%** — Invisible. Customers can barely find you. You're losing 80%+ of potential leads.
- **21-40%** — Barely visible. You get some organic traffic, but most searchers choose competitors.
- **41-60%** — Average. You're competitive but leaving easy wins on the table.
- **61-80%** — Strong. You're outperforming most competitors in your market.
- **81-100%** — Dominant. You own your local search market.

## How to Start Fixing It

The most effective approach is to fix issues in order of impact:

**Immediate wins (this week):**
- Enable SMS on your phone number
- Set up a direct review link and QR code
- Add 20 photos to your Google Business Profile

**Short-term fixes (1-2 weeks):**
- Migrate to a recognized hosting provider
- Install Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager
- Start responding to every review

**Long-term optimization (1-3 months):**
- Clean up directory listings across 15+ platforms
- Build review generation to 5-10 new reviews per month
- Install chat widget and conversion tracking
- Set up automated review responses

## Category 1: Business Details

### ❌ SMS Not Enabled
**9 out of 10 consumers** want to text businesses. If they can't text you, they call a competitor.

**The fix:** We set up an SMS-enabled number (Google Voice or dedicated line) and update it everywhere.

*Included in: Starter, Growth, Pro*

### ❌ Poor Website Hosting
Google penalizes sites it can't identify the host for. Mystery hosting = buried in search.

**The fix:** We migrate you to a recognized, fast host with SSL and speed optimization.

*Included in: Growth, Pro*

### ❌ No Chat Widget
Websites with live chat convert **40% more visitors**.

**The fix:** We install a professional chat widget with auto-greeting and after-hours responder.

*Included in: Pro*

### ❌ Not Responding to Reviews
**89% of consumers** prefer businesses that reply to reviews. Google also rewards responsiveness in rankings.

**The fix:** Our AI Review Responder drafts professional, personalized replies to every review within 24 hours.

*Included in: Starter, Growth, Pro*

## Category 2: Techno Stack

### ❌ No Google Analytics
You can't improve what you don't measure. Without analytics, you're flying blind.

**The fix:** Install GA4 with event tracking for calls, form submissions, and chat.

*Included in: Growth, Pro*

### ❌ No Google Tag Manager
GTM lets you manage all tracking codes without touching website code.

**The fix:** Install GTM and migrate all tracking (Analytics, Ads, Meta) into one dashboard.

*Included in: Growth, Pro*

### ❌ No Ads Conversion Tracking
If you run Google Ads without conversion tracking, **30-50% of your ad spend is wasted.**

**The fix:** Install Ads conversion tag + call tracking so every dollar is measured.

*Included in: Pro*

### ❌ No Meta Pixel
Without the Meta Pixel, you can't retarget website visitors or track Facebook ad performance.

**The fix:** Install Meta Pixel via GTM with event tracking.

*Included in: Growth, Pro*

Every unaddressed negative review costs you customers.
